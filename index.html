SEW YOU CLUB

About Us

We are here to spread DIY vibes. Have you ever dreamed about having unique pieces in your wardrobe? Did you consider sewing something yourself? It is easier and could be more entertaining than you think!

It all started in 2021, when two best friends wanted to meet more like-minded people - positive, creative and ready to learn sewing lingerie, but also to get some basics. Our dear Club is constantly growing and open for new members. We meet every weekend to sew, but also hang out after our workshop time - have a glass of wine or piece of snack.

"This Club is one of the best things I discovered after moving to Lisbon, when I was looking to meet new people. Now I always plan my saturdays to not miss weekly evening sew-events."

Maria - Software developer, joined 2022
